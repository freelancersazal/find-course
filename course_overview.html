About The Course

Bachelor in Islamic Finance is a growing industry in the financial world which is garnering interest from many sectors. Similar to conventional financial systems, Islamic finance features banks, capital markets, fund managers, investment firms and insurance companies. These entities are regulated by Islamic law and the finance industry rules and regulations which apply to their traditional counterparts. The Bachelor in Islamic Finance programme is geared to provide you with the fundamentals in Islamic finance focusing on a comprehensive exposure to its concepts, principles and practices. You will complement your study of Islamic finance with other courses such as economics, accounting and management. The built-in industry training will also give you valuable first-hand experience in the field.
